About QScore

QScore was built to encourage social distancing during the COVID-19 pandemic in a fun and unique way.

Scoring

Users can earn points by spending time within 150 meters of their home location. There are two scores: the all time score and the 24 hour score.

The all time score is determined by the total time spent at home and is continuously updated.

The 24-hour score is a rolling percentage of the time spent at home in the last 24 hours.

All time scores are public, and 24 hour scores are private.

Privacy

QScore requires your home location to calculate your score. Your home location is private and will never leave your device.
